ABOUT (ASSOCIATION OF ALL INDIA JEET KUNE-DO FOUNDATION ) AAIJF

  • 1. A Yearly workshop or Camp Should be Conducted by the Member districts.
  • 2. Every District should provide at least 15 students or more on mandatory basis.
  • 3. Yearly State Tournaments Should be Conducted in Every District in the Round-Robin Method.
  • 4. The selection Process for the Nationals Should be Conducted Each Year and those students got selected will be participate in National level Tournaments.
  • 5. Students who performs well in the state and national tournaments will be preferred for the “School of Games” conducted by central government of India.
  • 6. District membership should Renewed on March 31 of every year by paying ₹1000. If not, that particular district membership will be cancelled.
  • 7. Workshops ,Self defence camps and Referee camps and referee examination will be conducted every year.
  • 8. An member districts are asked to participate in the activities that are conducted by the state committee.
  • 9. Those who were certified in the referee examination will only be allowed who acted as referee in the state tournaments for 2years will be selected as national referees on quality basis.
  • 10. Problems in the committee or among the committee members should be outspoken in the state general body meeting Only. On any causes, no one approaches the central body.

Our Association of All India Jeet Kune-Do Foundation (AAIJF) has been working since 1980 in India and got registered in since 1990 through Society Registration Act 1860. From 1980 to till 1990 our organization organized many Jeet Kune-Do events. Our foundation is working in more than 25 States of India. In the year 2001, the Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangthan organized Vande Mataram Sports Meet. In this Meet our Jeet Kune-Do art was included as game and we organized Vande Mataram Jeet Kune-Do Championship. This championship was organized at University of Delhi(India). In the year 2003, the Indian Olympic Association and Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports were organized the Swabhiman Sports Festival-2003. In this festival our foundation was also a part of the mentioned festival and we again organized Jeet Kune-Do Championship under their banner .In this festival our president Master S. L. Gihara was awarded by Vice-President of India We submitted our file in the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports regarding recognition. We got affiliation from World Jeet Kune-Do Federation (USA) in 2003 and got recognition from Jeet Kune-Do Martial Arts- International in 2006

Our foundation participated in various JKD training Camps / Seminars / Workshops and Tournaments like 1st Bangkok Cup Jeet Kune-Do (Martial Arts) Tournament-2006 held at A Beg University of Thailand, Bangkok (Thailand). Our students won various medals and prizes in this championship. The World Martial Arts Organization awarded to our Master S. L. Gihara by Silver Medal Award for his 25years outstanding performance and supported performing in Jeet Kune-Do in Asia. Recognised by ISO(9001 : 2015) and Niti Aayog (Regd. under NGO Darpan, Unique ID VO/ NGO DL/2017/0154559) and (Jeet kundeo Rec.) School Games Federation of India (SGFI). Time to time our foundation organized various Inter School, Inter Club, Inter College, State, National and International level champhionship. Many time we received so many invitation letter from abroad.
